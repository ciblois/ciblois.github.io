I'm Cinthya Blois.

Junior Data Analyst

After 6 years of working in the academic field of Nanotechnology (MSc. and P.hD.), I decided to redirect my career to business intelligence & data analysis. I finished a Bootcamp on data analysis at Ironhack, Lisbon. Before that, I started studying data science tools on my own through online platforms and took small courses in Python, MySQL, statistics, HTML, and CSS. During the time I worked with research, I always worked with data collection and interpretation, however in a more technical and research-oriented way.

My apprenticeship at Ironhack has allowed me to develop my data skills: Data Visualization using Tableau and Power BI; data mining using Python, Numpy, Pandas, SciPy, Matplot and Seaborn; Data analysis through statistics with SciPy or through Machine Learning predictions with sklearn; Database using MySQL Workbench. My background as a researcher has allowed me to develop the quality of paying attention to detail, working with deadlines and projects, and also adapting to multicultural environments.
